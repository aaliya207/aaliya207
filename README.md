# 💫 About Me:
Hey, I’m Aaliya Patel , A computer science student currently diving deep into cybersecurity and how systems actually break (and how to stop that from happening). I like understanding things beyond the surface level , not just how to build them, but how they behave under pressure, where they fail, and how to make them stronger.<br>Right now, I’m exploring areas like system security, basic penetration testing concepts, and how real-world applications are structured and exploited. I also build projects along the way , sometimes just for my own use , as a way to understand architecture, workflows, and practical implementation in a more hands-on way.<br>I’m always open to collaborating on projects where I can learn something new, especially anything related to backend systems, security testing, or real-world application scenarios. At the same time, I’m actively learning and improving, so I’m always open to guidance, better approaches, and deeper insights.<br>If you’re into breaking things (ethically), securing them, or just understanding how everything works under the hood, we’ll probably get along.<br>And yeah, I tend to stick with things until they actually work the way they’re supposed to.
